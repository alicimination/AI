# Domain and Validity Constraints

## Common domain checks
- Denominator must be non-zero.
- Even root radicand must be non-negative.
- Logarithm argument must be positive.
- Inverse trig inputs must lie in allowed range.

## Probability-specific checks
- Probability value must be between 0 and 1 inclusive.
- Total outcomes must be non-zero and well-defined.

## Calculus checks
- For optimization, verify candidate points and boundaries.
- For limits, one-sided behavior may differ.

## Pitfalls
- Reporting algebraic roots without filtering by domain.
- Ignoring interval constraints given in question.
