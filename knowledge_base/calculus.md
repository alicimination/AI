# Calculus Basics (JEE)

## Derivatives
- `d/dx (x^n) = n*x^(n-1)`.
- Product rule: `(uv)' = u'v + uv'`.
- Chain rule: `(f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x))*g'(x)`.

## Integrals
- `integral x^n dx = x^(n+1)/(n+1), n!=-1`.
- `integral 1/x dx = ln|x| + C`.

## Common Mistakes
- Missing constant of integration.
- Ignoring domain for log/trig transforms.
