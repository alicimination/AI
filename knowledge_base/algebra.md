# Algebra Essentials (JEE)

## Core Formulas
- Quadratic roots: for `ax^2+bx+c=0`, `x = (-b ± sqrt(b^2-4ac)) / 2a`.
- Sum/product of roots: `alpha + beta = -b/a`, `alpha*beta = c/a`.
- Identities: `(a+b)^2`, `(a-b)^2`, `a^2-b^2`.

## Typical Approach
1. Standardize equation.
2. Factorize or apply quadratic formula.
3. Verify roots in constraints.

## Common Mistakes
- Sign error in discriminant.
- Ignoring domain constraints like denominator `!= 0`.
