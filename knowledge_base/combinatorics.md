# Combinatorics Quick Guide (JEE Level)

## Core counting rules
- Addition rule: if tasks are mutually exclusive, total ways = sum.
- Multiplication rule: if tasks happen in sequence, total ways = product.
- Complement counting: favorable = total - unfavorable.

## Permutations and combinations
- Permutations: nPr = n! / (n-r)!
- Combinations: nCr = n! / (r!(n-r)!)
- Circular permutations (distinct objects): (n-1)!

## Typical patterns
1. Arrange letters with repeats: n!/(a!b!c!...)
2. Choose committees with restrictions: split into cases and add.
3. At least / at most constraints: often easiest with complement.

## Pitfalls
- Mixing order-sensitive and order-insensitive counting.
- Forgetting repeated objects reduce permutation count.
- Forgetting to divide by symmetry in circular seating.
